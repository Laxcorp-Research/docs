Every recording in Raven is a **session**. Sessions capture your transcript, AI responses, and metadata - and they're automatically saved so you can review them later.

## Session History

After a recording ends, the session appears in your **dashboard** with:

- An **AI-generated title** based on the conversation content
- The **duration** of the recording
- The **mode** that was active
- A **timestamp** of when it happened

Click any session to see its full details.

## Session Detail View

Each session has several tabs:

| Tab | What it shows |
|-----|--------------|
| **Summary** | AI-generated markdown summary with sections like Key Decisions, Action Items, and Topics Discussed. The title is editable. You can click "Regenerate Summary" to get a fresh one. |
| **Transcript** | Full transcript with "You" and "Them" speaker labels and timestamps. |
| **Chat** | All AI questions and responses from that session, preserved as a conversation thread. |
| **Insights** (Pro) | Post-meeting analysis: summary, action items, topics, sentiment, and key phrases. |

## Copying Content

Each tab has copy-to-clipboard options:

- **Summary**: Copy the full summary as markdown
- **Transcript**: Choose "My transcript", "Their transcript", or "Full transcript"
- **Chat**: Copy individual AI responses
- **Insights**: Copy individual sections or all insights at once

## Search

The dashboard header has a **search bar** that lets you find past sessions by keyword. Search matches against session titles, summaries, and transcript text. Results appear as a list of matching sessions you can click into.

## Auto-Save

Sessions auto-save every 60 seconds during recording. If Raven crashes or your computer shuts down unexpectedly, you won't lose more than a minute of data. On next launch, any interrupted session is recovered and marked as "Recovered Session."

## Incognito Mode

Toggle **incognito** on the overlay pill (hat-and-glasses icon) before starting a recording. Incognito sessions:

- Are **not saved** to your session history
- Are **not synced** to the cloud (Pro)
- Disappear completely when the recording ends

Use this for sensitive conversations you don't want persisted.

## Post-Meeting Insights (Pro)

Raven Pro users with a paid subscription can generate post-meeting insights on completed sessions. Open a session and click the **Insights** tab, then **Generate Insights**.

The analysis includes:

| Insight | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Summary** | Bullet-point overview of the meeting |
| **Action Items** | Specific tasks and follow-ups mentioned |
| **Topics** | Key subjects discussed |
| **Sentiment** | Tone analysis per speaker |
| **Key Phrases** | Important terms and phrases |

## Cloud Sync (Pro)

In Raven Pro, sessions automatically sync to the cloud:

- Your session history is available on any device you sign into
- AI chat messages within sessions are preserved across devices
- Syncing happens automatically after each session ends and periodically in the background
- If there's a conflict (same session edited on two devices), the most recent edit wins

In the open-source version, all sessions are stored locally on your machine.
