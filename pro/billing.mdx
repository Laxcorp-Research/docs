<Note>
This page is about **Raven Pro** plans. The [open-source version](/quickstart/overview) has no plans or limits - you use your own API keys with no restrictions.
</Note>

## Plans

| | Free | Pro Monthly | Pro Yearly |
|--|------|-------------|------------|
| **Price** | $0 | $29.99/mo | $24.99/mo ($299.99/yr) |
| **AI requests/day** | 5 | Unlimited | Unlimited |
| **Session duration** | 2 minutes | Unlimited | Unlimited |
| **Post-meeting insights** | -- | Yes | Yes |
| **Cloud sync** | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| **Meeting detection** | Yes | Yes | Yes |

All limits on the Free tier reset at **midnight UTC** each day.

## Upgrading to Pro

When you're ready to upgrade:

1. Click **"Upgrade Now"** in the overlay (appears when you hit a limit) or go to **Settings**
2. You'll be taken to a checkout page to enter payment details
3. After payment, your plan updates immediately
4. All limits are removed - unlimited AI, unlimited session duration

## Managing Your Subscription

From the **Settings** page, click **Manage Subscription** to open your billing portal. There you can:

- Update your payment method
- Switch between monthly and yearly billing
- View past invoices
- Cancel your subscription

## What Happens When You Hit a Limit

### AI request limit (5/day on Free)

When you've used all 5 AI responses for the day:

- A banner appears: **"You've used all 5 free AI responses for today"**
- The AI stops responding until the limit resets at midnight UTC
- An **"Upgrade Now"** button lets you remove the limit instantly

### Session duration limit (2 minutes on Free)

When a recording reaches the 2-minute mark:

- The recording **automatically stops**
- A banner appears: **"Free sessions are limited to 2 minutes"**
- Your transcript from those 2 minutes is fully preserved
- An **"Upgrade Now"** button lets you record without limits

### Offline grace

If Raven can't reach the server when starting a session, it allows one **grace session** (limited to 2 minutes) so you're never completely blocked.
